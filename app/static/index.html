Resume Organization

We provide an organize system of managing your resumes, with fast and easy way to upload and and compare between each version.

Resume Analysis

By utilizing information retrieval and active learning algorithms, we were able to create a analysis report for your resume containing extracted keywords and protential positions. Additionally, these informations are used for our job recommendation system.

Job recommendation

Our system will recommend related positions for you based on your uploaded resume, selected location, and experience. You can either apply directly, or bookmark a job in the job list.

This is a graduate-level course covering the advanced topics in the growing field of information retrieval (IR) where the goal is to study how to build intelligent software tools to help users management and make use of large amounts of unstructured (typically textual) data.
